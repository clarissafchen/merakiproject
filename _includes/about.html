About This Project

As a new designer, sometimes experience is not going to make it the cut to get the job I want.

This is why I have created this project for my Cisco Meraki job application.

I want to demonstrate that I am a capable and dedicated individual and that, even though I don't have the years of experience, the heart and passion are definitely there.

For this project, I decided to do a makeover on the Cisco Meraki logo as well as your Network Dashboard.
