About This Project

As a new designer, sometimes experience is not going to make the cut to get the job I want.

This is why I have created this project for my Cisco Meraki job application.

I want to demonstrate that I am a capable and dedicated individual and that, even though I don't have the years of experience, the heart and passion are definitely there.

For this project, I decided to do a makeover on the Cisco Meraki logo as well as your Network Dashboard.



As a first step, I researched trends in design that could be used for this makeover.

Then I mapped out the ideas in a diagram and made notes of what I wanted to use.



After a few rough drafts, the whole process yielded the results bellow.

A new logo for Cisco Meraki that puts the main company (Cisco) and the branch (Meraki) together as opposed to separate things.

A cleaner, more modern dashboard with humanist and modern typefaces for ease-of-reading and bright colors for accents.
